About Jarrah Build

The residential construction experts

Who are we?

Jarrah build is a progressive residential building company specializing in all building and carpentry work. Founding director Jared Edwards prides himself on prioritizing the clients experience and making sure every detail is perfected to contribute to the visions and dreams of his clients and projects. Jarrah build collaborates with a team of skilled trades to produce seamless builds, clear communication and reliability.
